I have been accepted to DevPoint Labs' Ruby on Rails web development bootcamp for the Spring 2017 cohort. I was awarded the Hopper Scholarship, which is so exciting, but there is a lot pressure that comes with such a gift. I am terrified and excited to take on this new adventure!

So, where am I going? I really don't know, but I do know it's going to be great.

My goal is to complement my existing skill set to move up in the world and gain complete independence. I am so much stronger than I ever imagined I could be and now I am ready to create a life for myself and my kids that mirrors this.

My coding background is pretty shoddy and to call it out-of-date is a significant understatement. However, when I learned Pascal in 8th grade, I was the top of my class and the same the semester I took Lisp in college. I think I have a coder's brain. We're about to find out!

I really feel like this is finally going to be my year and it all starts with Ruby on Rails under the tutelage of DPL.
