First and foremost I am the obsessive mother of two dogs, Guy and Cooper. You can learn all about them here.

I was born and raised in Salt Lake City, Utah, to Ron and Sally Larkin. I have two older biological brothers, a younger brother who is handicapped, and four Tongan brothers. I am the only girl and I love that.

I graduated from the Waterford School in 1997 and headed to upstate NY where I attended Vassar College and had more fun than I can publish online. I graduated in Studio Art, which was amazing and did not prepare me for a career. Or maybe I didn't capitalize on my skill set quickly enough. Either way, it has led me to expand my capabilities with the complement of web development at DevPoint Labs. You can read more about this on my LinkedIn profile.

I am addicted to music. '80s New Wave and Synthpop are easily my favourite genres and The Smiths are my favourite band. I also love Phil Collins, Taylor Swift, and One Direction. 1D have been my current fave for several years now and I'm probably the oldest person in their fanbase.

I live for the ballet and danced for 18 years with a hiatus during high school. I am a rapacious reader with a penchant for Dostoevsky and Sholokhov as well as the Sherlock Holmes anthology. I love crime dramas and horror movies. Finally, I love to run, or moving mediatation as I genreally refer to it. I live with an overactive brain and while I can't shut in off whilst running, I can't hold on to a thought long enough to ruminate.

Everything is better with champagne!
